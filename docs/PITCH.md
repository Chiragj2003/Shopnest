# Shopnest — Pitch Deck

## Slide 1 · Title
**Shopnest** — Your Instagram shop, now a real storefront.
*One link. Every product. Orders on WhatsApp. Paid via UPI.*

## Slide 2 · Problem
India's small sellers run entire businesses inside Instagram DMs:
- Price lists screenshot-ed and re-sent 50 times a day
- "Is this available?" → manual back-and-forth for every order
- No catalog, no order trail, no idea which product actually sells
- Marketplaces (Amazon/Meesho) take commissions and bury their brand

## Slide 3 · Solution
A mobile-first one-page store, live in 5 minutes:
- Buyers browse, tap products into a cart, and checkout sends **one WhatsApp message** with the full order
- **UPI QR with the cart total pre-filled** — no payment gateway, no KYC, no commission
- Hindi/English storefront, beautiful themes, drag-and-drop builder

## Slide 4 · Product (demo slide)
- Seller dashboard: products, store builder with live phone preview, analytics
- Instagram import: paste a post URL → product draft
- Growth kit: PDF catalog + Instagram story card with store QR
- *Demo: shopnest.store/demobakery*

## Slide 5 · Why now / Market
- ~60–70M MSMEs in India; social commerce projected in the tens of billions of dollars this decade (Bain x Sequoia estimates)
- WhatsApp (500M+ users in India) and UPI (13B+ txns/month) are already the default rails — Shopnest just connects them
- Sellers don't want another marketplace; they want **their own** store on rails they trust

## Slide 6 · Business model
Freemium SaaS, priced for impulse-buy via UPI:
- **Free:** 10 products, full storefront, WhatsApp orders
- **Premium ₹199/mo or ₹999/yr:** unlimited products, deeper analytics, premium themes, no "Powered by Shopnest" badge
- Launch offer: **Lifetime ₹1,499** for first 100 sellers (cash + testimonials + case studies)

## Slide 8 · Go-to-market
1. **Niche-first:** home bakers → then thrift/jewelry/boutiques (high Instagram density, photogenic products)
2. **Communities:** Facebook/WhatsApp/Telegram groups of home bakers & resellers; college fests and flea markets
3. **Built-in virality:** every storefront footer, catalog PDF, and story card carries the Shopnest QR/link
4. **Content:** reels — "DM seller vs Shopnest seller", "store live in 5 minutes"
5. **Micro-influencers:** free lifetime Premium to 20 seller-creators for story shoutouts

## Slide 9 · Traction & roadmap
- ✅ Product built and live (storefront, builder, analytics, share kit)
- Next 90 days: 100 active stores → measure WhatsApp-click-through and weekly active sellers
- Roadmap: order status tracking, Razorpay payment-link upsell, themes marketplace, reseller referral program

## Slide 10 · Ask
- **To sellers:** your store free in 5 minutes — link in bio
- **To angels/incubators:** ₹25–50L pre-seed for 12 months: founder + 1 growth hire + ads experiment to find CAC < 3 months of LTV
